Welcome

Hey there, I am excited to welcome you to my world. I call this my world because it contains all that you would need to know about me. For a long time, I was always confused how to answer this simple question “Who are you and what do you do?” Why – because I was a lot of things in world where you needed to be known for one thing (or so I thought). From being a dancer – speaker – media enthusiast – photographer – geologist, what was I really supposed to define myself as? So right now, I am super glad to be able to present the different facets of my life to you. Once again, I welcome you to my world!

I was recently part of a phone photography exhibition organized by Ivy events in the Abuja, Nigeria. At the shehu Musa Yaradua center. The event that held on the 23rd of March was put together to raise funds for a charity organization that caters to the needs of widows and their children. The charity organization known as Ashake foundation started a widow’s business school initiative, which is an amazing initiative.

So I thought to exhibit alongside some other phone photographers’ images that would not only raise funds for the foundation but also inspire others to appreciate and maximize their mobile devices when looking to capture great moments.
